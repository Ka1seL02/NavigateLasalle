DLSU-D joins Study Abroad Fair in Taiwan

March 14, 2024 News

DLSU-D’s Linkages and Scholarship Office recently participated in the Partner Day and Study Abroad Fair organized by Providence University in Taiwan, aiming to enhance the presence of foreign students at DLSU-D through its regular program offerings and exchange programs, with Dr. Sonia Gementiza, Dr. George Francisco, and Mr. Romano Angelico Ebron representing the university, which was the only Philippine institution to present its global initiatives to various foreign institutions during the two-day event that highlighted the university’s internationalization programs, including academic mobility, foreign admissions, and social events among university officials, diplomatic corps, and other stakeholders—Animo La Salle!
