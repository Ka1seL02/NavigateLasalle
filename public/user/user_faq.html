Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Navigate La Salle.

What is Navigate La Salle?

Navigate La Salle is an interactive Information and Wayfinding Kiosk system developed to enhance navigation within DLSU-D. It features a dynamic interactive map with wayfinding capabilities, a 360-degree virtual campus tour, and a news and events calendar — all designed to improve accessibility, engagement, and technological integration within the university community.

Is the kiosk available 24/7?

The kiosk is available during campus operational hours. Availability may vary depending on building schedules, maintenance, and campus events.

Can I access the kiosk services remotely (e.g., through a website or app)?

Navigate La Salle is currently designed for on-campus access through kiosks. Remote access via a web portal or mobile application is under consideration for future development.

What areas or buildings are covered by the kiosk system?

The system covers key areas of DLSU-D, including academic buildings, administrative offices, libraries, cafeterias, clinics, recreational spaces, and notable landmarks across the campus.

How accurate are the directions provided by the kiosk?

Navigate La Salle provides highly accurate, optimized, and efficient routes across the DLSU-D campus, always guiding you through the fastest and most convenient paths available.

Does the map show real-time information, like construction zones or closed pathways?

Currently, the map does not display real-time construction zones or temporary closures. Future updates may integrate these features for even better navigation.

How do I use the interactive map?

Users can easily search for locations, view campus maps, and get step-by-step directions by following on-screen prompts. The interface is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, requiring no specific peripherals or complex interactions.

Can I get directions to specific buildings/locations on campus?

Yes. Navigate La Salle allows users to search for specific destinations and provides detailed routing instructions across the campus.

How do I report a technical issue with the kiosk?

To report a technical issue, users can use the “Provide Feedback” button on the kiosk dashboard. You can enter your name (optional), email (optional), user type (visitor, student, faculty, etc.), and a description of the issue encountered.

How can I provide feedback about my experience with the kiosk?

Feedback can be submitted through the “Provide Feedback” section on the kiosk. Users can optionally include their name and email address, select their role (e.g., visitor, student, faculty), and share their comments or suggestions.

How often is the information updated on the kiosk?

Information displayed on Navigate La Salle is updated regularly by system administrators to ensure accuracy and relevance for all users.

Can I print directions or information from the kiosk?

Printing is not available. However, users are welcome to take a picture of the displayed information. All content shown is intended for public view, and no sensitive data is presented on the kiosks.
