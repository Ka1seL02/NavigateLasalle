De La Salle University–Dasmariñas (DLSU-D) stands as the leading Lasallian institution in Cavite, committed to delivering transformative, values-centered education. As the largest university in the province, DLSU-D provides a wide range of academic programs that nurture critical thinking, creativity, and professional excellence across various disciplines.

Guided by its Lasallian mission, the university promotes holistic student development through dynamic campus life, extensive student organizations, and opportunities for leadership, research, and global engagement. DLSU-D is also recognized for its strong advocacy for environmental stewardship, sustainability, and community service—cultivating graduates who embody integrity, compassion, and social responsibility.

Rooted in faith and service, DLSU-D continues to empower students to become competent professionals, ethical innovators, and active contributors to both national progress and the global community.

Our Journey

History of DLSU-D

1977

Foundation of GEAC-Cavite

On July 18, 1977, the institution was established as General Emilio Aguinaldo College (GEAC)—Cavite, under the management of Dr. Paulo C. Campos and the Yaman Lahi Foundation. It offered courses in education, business administration, nursing, midwifery, and allied health fields, marking the beginning of its journey in Cavite.

1978

Early Growth

The small library of GEAC, initially just a classroom, was moved to the administration building. This laid the foundation for a more formal information resource center.

1980

Expansion of Technical Courses

Technical and vocational courses were added to the curriculum, strengthening the college's role in practical training.

1987

Lasallian Integration

GEAC was transferred under the management of the Frère Benilde Romançon Educational Foundation, a sister corporation of De La Salle University.

1994

Institutional Development

The Aklatang Emilio Aguinaldo (library) was inaugurated, reflecting growth in academic infrastructure.

2000

Founding Beginnings (Lasallian Era)

By 2000, DLSU-D had matured under Lasallian management, preparing for greater autonomy as a leading Lasallian university in Cavite.

2010

Recognition & Excellence

DLSU-D achieved recognition as a Center of Excellence in the College of Education and Level IV accreditation.

Today

Present-Day DLSU-D

Today, De La Salle University–Dasmariñas is a fully established Lasallian Catholic university in Cavite, emphasizing faith, zeal, and communion.
