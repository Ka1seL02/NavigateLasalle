I recently had the opportunity to explore the interactive map and virtual tour features of your platform, and I must say it was an incredibly immersive experience. The interface is very intuitive, allowing me to navigate through different areas effortlessly. I especially appreciated the level of detail provided for each location — from the historical background to the key facilities, everything was clearly documented and visually engaging. However, I did notice a few areas where improvements could be made. For instance, some of the interactive elements took a few moments to load, which slightly interrupted the flow of the tour. Additionally, while the search functionality is robust, it would be helpful if there were filters or tags that allow users to narrow down points of interest based on categories or accessibility options. Overall, I am impressed with the platform’s ability to combine educational content with an interactive experience. It is clear that a lot of effort has gone into both the visual design and the accuracy of the information. I would highly recommend adding more multimedia elements, such as short videos or audio guides, to further enrich the user experience. Keep up the excellent work!
